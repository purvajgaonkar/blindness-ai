How RetinalAI Works

Four intelligent steps transform your retinal image into a complete clinical diagnosis

01

Upload Retinal Image

Drag and drop or select a retinal fundus photograph. Supports PNG, JPEG formats from standard fundus cameras.

02

AI Preprocessing

Ben Graham preprocessing, CLAHE-LAB contrast enhancement, and non-local means denoising prepare the image for analysis.

03

Vessel & Lesion Analysis

Frangi vesselness filter segments blood vessels. Morphological analysis detects exudates, hemorrhages, and microaneurysms.

04

Diagnosis & Advice

EfficientNet-B4 classifies DR severity (0–4). Grad-CAM visualises the decision. Evidence-based recommendations are generated.

Medical Background

About Diabetic Retinopathy

The leading cause of preventable blindness worldwide — and why early detection matters

Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is a diabetes complication that affects the eyes. It is caused by damage to the blood vessels of the retina — the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye. DR affects approximately 1 in 3 people with diabetes, and is the leading cause of blindness in working-age adults. Early detection through regular fundus photography screening can prevent up to 95% of DR-related blindness.

Disease Progression: 5 Stages of DR

Grade 0
No DR
Normal retina. No diabetic changes visible. Annual monitoring.
Grade 1
Mild
Microaneurysms only. Small balloon-like swellings in blood vessels.
Grade 2
Moderate
More vessels blocked. Hemorrhages and exudates accumulate.
Grade 3
Severe
Many blocked vessels. Retina signals growth of abnormal vessels. High risk.
Grade 4
Proliferative
New abnormal vessels grow and bleed. Vitreous hemorrhage. Blindness risk.

Technology Stack

EfficientNet-B4
Google's compound-scaled CNN. 1792-d feature embeddings for 5-class DR grading.
Grad-CAM
Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping. Makes AI decisions interpretable for clinicians.
CLAHE + Ben Graham
Competition-winning preprocessing. Removes illumination variation, enhances vessels.
Frangi Filter
Hessian eigenvalue analysis for gold-standard blood vessel segmentation.
